Brief

Lloyds banking group is the the UK’s largest financial services group which also includes Halifax, Bank of Scotland, and Scottish Widows. 

I was hired by LLoyds to help replatform and align the design accross the existing mobile banking apps and to design and prototype the next generation apps to incroporate open-banking.

I took part in a number of hack days where I created prototypes for a Google Wear android app and banking concepts for customers with mental health issues.
